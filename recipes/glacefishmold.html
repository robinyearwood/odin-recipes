Glace Fish Mold

Description

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup cold water
  • 1 envelope plain gelatin
  • 1 cup boiling water
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 3 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 cup flaked fish
  • 1/4 cup diced green pepper
  • 1/2 unpeeled cucumber, cut
  • 1 small onion, sliced
  • 1/2 cup chopped pimiento
  • 1/4 cup diced celery

Steps

  1. Soften gelatine in cold water.
  2. Dissolve in boiling water.
  3. Add sugar, salt, lemon juice.
  4. Cool.
  5. When mixture begins to thicken, fold in fish, green pepper, cucumber wedges, onion rings, pimiento and celery
  6. Pour into 1-quart mold, rinsed in cold water.
  7. When firm, unmold, garnish as desired, and serve with a bowl of real mayonnaise.
